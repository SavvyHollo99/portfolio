About Me

I graduated with a B.S. in E-commerce and minors in Information Technology, Psychology, and Marketing. I have experience working for a large-scale corporate company; a university-based research institution; and a small, low-budget non-profit. I look for positions that challenge me creatively and provide opportunities for growth.

Detailed PDF Resume

I am imaginative.

As an only child and a habitual bookworm, my mind is a world of its own. I love to brainstorm ideas and have often found myself engaged in good-natured, fruitful debates. My imagination hones my design skills and helps me produce web projects varying in style and purpose; it sparks inspiration when I need to develop content for diverse target audiences; it is the reason I flourish in creative environments and I consider it to be one of my strongest assets.

I am industrious.

Maybe it is because procrastination gives me anxiety, but I find that working smart and getting things done go hand and hand with success. In high school, I was Student Government President and Valedictorian. In my time at U.A. Little Rock, I was on the Chancellor’s List every semester, received awards in both my E-commerce major and my I.T. minor, was inducted into the Phi Kappa Phi Honor Society, and graduated Summa Cum Laude.

I am resourceful.

Projects - no matter the size - come with their fair share of obstacles. Whether it is developing a digital identity for a budding research institute or finding a way to build a WordPress site with a budget of $0, I have had to research, negotiate, and stretch my way around many hurdles.
